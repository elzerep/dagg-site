Technology

The Intelligence Layer — your company context, made operable by agents.

Generic models commoditize. Company-specific context does not. Dagg's intelligence layer combines knowledge graphs, retrieval, permissions, audit trails and workflow context — structuring how your work, knowledge, systems and decisions connect, so every agent and workflow acts with real understanding of your business.

Under the hood it's a proprietary method: a knowledge graph plus retrieval, bound to live workflow context. The more you run it, the more it knows — and the harder it becomes to replace.
